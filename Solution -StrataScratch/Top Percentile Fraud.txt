Top Percentile Fraud

SQL  :

SELECT 
        policy_num,
        state, 
        claim_cost,
        fraud_score, 
        NTILE(20) OVER (PARTITION BY state ORDER BY fraud_score DESC) AS rnk 
    FROM fraud_score
